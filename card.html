Pembroke Welsh Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is known as one of the most agreeable of small house dogs and is a loyal, loving pet for families.

  • Origin: Wales
  • Weight: 14 kg (31 lb)
  • Height: 25–30 cm (9.8–11.8 in)
  • Average Life Span: 12 years and 3 months

The Corgi name originated from the Welshmen who developed the breed and called them “dwarf dog” due to their stature. In Welsh, “cor” means dwarf and “gi” means dog. Corgis were prized in early Welsh settlements for their loyalty, herding abilities, and companionship.
